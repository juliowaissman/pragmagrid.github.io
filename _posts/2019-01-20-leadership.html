Governance

PRAGMA is led by a Steering Committee and its Executive Committee, which operate according to the Operating Principles and Procedures document, which all PRAGMA members agree to abide by. The Steering Committee sets direction, reviews membership applications and proposals to host PRAGMA workshops.
